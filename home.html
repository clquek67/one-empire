FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about building your one-person empire with AI.

What is One Empire?

One Empire is the operating system for solo entrepreneurs — a platform of AI-powered tools that lets one person run a business at company scale, without a team. Each tool in the Empire Stack handles a different function of your business.

Who is One Empire built for?

Solo founders, independent consultants, coaches, and corporate professionals going out on their own. Anyone running a one-person business who wants to operate at company scale without the company overhead.

What does AI Content Studio do?

You submit one weekly brief. Claude AI generates platform-native content for LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and TikTok — then auto-publishes everything. No manual work, no copy-pasting, no reformatting.

How does Empire PM work?

Empire PM is AI-powered project management built for freelancers and small agencies. It handles task planning, client portals, time tracking, and automated invoicing — so you run client work like a studio, not a spreadsheet.

Can I maintain my own voice in AI-generated content?

Yes. AI Content Studio includes brand voice training so every piece of generated content stays consistent with how you naturally communicate — your tone, your style, your authority.

What platforms does One Empire publish to?

LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and TikTok. All from a single weekly brief — no copy-pasting, no reformatting, no manual scheduling.
