Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how One Empire ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using our content automation platform at one-empire.com and our member portal at brief.one-empire.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Content Brief Data: When you submit a weekly content brief, we collect your name, email address, business details, content topics, goals, and any other information you include in your brief.
  • Account Information: Your email address is used as your unique member identifier across our platform.

1.2 Social Media Authorization Data

When you connect your social media accounts to One Empire for automated publishing, we collect and store:

  • LinkedIn: Your LinkedIn member ID, display name, and OAuth access token (used to publish content on your behalf).
  • Facebook: Your Facebook user ID, name, page IDs, page names, and OAuth access token.
  • Instagram: Your Instagram Business Account ID, username, and OAuth access token.
  • YouTube: Your YouTube channel ID, channel name, and OAuth access token including refresh token.
  • TikTok: Your TikTok open ID, display name, and OAuth access token including refresh token.

We store access tokens securely in our database solely for the purpose of publishing content you have approved. We never store your social media passwords.

1.3 Content We Generate

We store AI-generated content created from your brief, including LinkedIn posts, carousels, video scripts, podcast show notes, and other formats. This content is stored in a queue until it is published or you request its deletion.

1.4 Usage and Technical Data

We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps solely for security and operational purposes. We do not use tracking pixels or behavioural advertising technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered content based on your brief using Anthropic Claude.
  • To deliver your generated content by email.
  • To automatically publish approved content to your connected social media accounts on your chosen schedule.
  • To notify you of publishing activity or issues with your connected accounts.
  • To maintain the security and functionality of our platform.
  • To communicate with you about your membership, service updates, and important notices.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate One Empire. Each has its own privacy policy governing their use of data:

3.1 AI Content Generation

  • Anthropic (Claude): Your content brief is sent to Anthropic's API to generate content. Anthropic's Privacy Policy governs their processing: anthropic.com/privacy

3.2 Database and Infrastructure

  • Supabase: Your data, including social media tokens and content queue, is stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database. Data is hosted in the region you select at signup. supabase.com/privacy
  • n8n (self-hosted): Our automation workflows run on a self-hosted n8n instance on a private VPS. No workflow data is sent to n8n's cloud.

3.3 Social Media Platforms

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram): facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • LinkedIn: linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • YouTube / Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

3.4 Email Delivery

  • Gmail / Google Workspace: We use Gmail to deliver your generated content by email.

4. Data Retention

  • Content queue items: Retained for 90 days after the scheduled publish date, then automatically deleted.
  • Social media tokens: Retained while your account is active. Tokens are invalidated and deleted when you disconnect a social account or close your account.
  • Content briefs: Retained for 12 months after submission.
  • Account data: Retained while your membership is active and for 30 days after cancellation.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS.
  • Social media access tokens are stored encrypted at rest in our database.
  • Access to our infrastructure is restricted by SSH key authentication.
  • We do not store social media passwords under any circumstances.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Revoke social media access: Disconnect any connected social account at any time through your account settings or directly through the social platform.
  • Data portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@one-empire.com.

7. Revoking Social Media Permissions

You can revoke One Empire's access to your social media accounts at any time:

  • Facebook & Instagram: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Security → Apps and Websites → Remove One Empire Social.
  • LinkedIn: Me → Settings & Privacy → Data privacy → Other applications → Permitted Services.
  • YouTube: Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access → Remove One Empire.
  • TikTok: Profile → Settings → Security → Authorised Apps → Revoke One Empire.

8. Children's Privacy

One Empire is a professional business service not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Our infrastructure is hosted in Europe. If you are accessing One Empire from outside the European Economic Area, your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. By using our service, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email and by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of One Empire after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
